Enterprise Cloud & GenAI Transformation

My Approach

Cummins Inc.

Automation Center of Excellence

2021 – 2024

$5M+ savings delivered 20% productivity gain 18% revenue increase 30% dev time reduction

The Challenge

Business IT had fragmented automation efforts — no governance, no reusability, no clear ownership. Point solutions were being built in silos, creating technical debt faster than the portfolio could absorb.

My Approach

I directed the Automation CoE from design through delivery — migrating on-premise RPA infrastructure to cloud platforms, implementing UiPath Orchestrator and Automation Hub for centralized governance, and building a reusable component factory that standardized practices across the portfolio. A flagship initiative was an Intelligent Document Processing solution using RPA and Azure Cognitive Services that automated high-volume document workflows end to end.

Outcome

$5M+ in measurable cost savings, 20% productivity improvement across Business IT, and an 18% increase in business revenue attributed to the IDP implementation.

Cummins Inc.

SharePoint Modernization at Scale

2019 – 2021

5,000+ sites migrated Global platform unified Cloud First strategy enabled

The Challenge

Cummins operated 5,000+ legacy SharePoint 2016 sites on-premise — a collaboration platform that had become a liability. Custom code, inconsistent governance, and aging infrastructure were blocking the Cloud First strategy.

My Approach

I architected the end-to-end migration program using a multi-release delivery model, moving all sites to SharePoint Online while simultaneously modernizing the platform experience. I championed a UI-first approach, designed reusable component libraries, and delivered consistent responsive experiences across desktop, mobile, and tablet.

Outcome

All 5,000+ sites successfully migrated, global collaboration unified on a single modern platform, and the legacy on-premise infrastructure decommissioned — enabling Cummins' Cloud First strategy at enterprise scale.
